Blow That Money — Privacy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

1. Overview

Blow That Money is a single-player game in which you upload screenshots of items and race to spend a fictional budget. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it is used, and your choices.

2. INFORMATION YOU PROVIDE

Photos you upload, item names and prices you type, your run configuration (budget tier, timer, currency), and any saved past runs you choose to keep.

3. INFORMATION COLLECTED AUTOMATICALLY

Anonymous usage events (screen views, run starts, run completions) tied only to a randomly generated device identifier — never your name, email, or Apple ID. You can opt out of analytics at any time from Settings.

4. WHAT WE DO NOT COLLECT

No accounts. No name, email, password, address, phone number, contacts, calendar, location, health, or financial information. No advertising identifiers. No ads.

5. PHOTO PROCESSING

When you upload a photo, it is sent over an encrypted connection to our backend, which forwards it to Anthropic Claude for two purposes: (1) reading the price and item name from the image (OCR), and (2) screening the image against a fixed list of disallowed content categories (recognizable people, illegal drugs, nudity, gore, hate symbols). Photos are not retained on our servers after processing. Anthropic is contractually prohibited from training on your inputs.

6. STORAGE AND RETENTION

Photos and saved runs live only inside the app on your device. Backend logs are kept up to 30 days for debugging and contain no personally identifying information. Anonymous analytics events are retained up to 24 months.

7. SHARING

We share information only with the service providers needed to operate the app: Anthropic (OCR and moderation), PostHog (anonymous analytics), and Apple (app distribution). We do not sell, rent, or trade your information to anyone. We will disclose information only when required by law.

8. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES Delete a saved run from inside the app, or uninstall the app to remove all local data. Disable anonymous analytics from Settings at any time. Revoke photo or camera access from iOS Settings. EU/UK/California residents have additional rights under GDPR/UK GDPR/CCPA — contact us to exercise them.

9. CHILDREN

The app is rated 17+ and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. SECURITY

We use TLS encryption in transit and standard measures at rest. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

11. CHANGES

We may update this policy. The date at the top will reflect any change. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app notice before they take effect.

Contact: support@younglobal.com
